Idli and Litti Chokha Recipe

Idli Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 2 cups idli rice (or parboiled rice)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions:

  1. Soak urad dal and fenugreek seeds in water for 4-6 hours.
  2. Soak the rice separately for the same duration.
  3. Drain the soaked dal and rice, and blend them separately to a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  4. Mix both batters together in a large bowl and add salt.
  5. Cover and let it ferment overnight or for 8-12 hours.
  6. Once fermented, pour the batter into idli molds and steam for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambhar.

Litti Chokha Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For Litti:
    • 2 cups whole wheat flour
    • 1/2 cup sattu (roasted gram flour)
    • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
    • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
    • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
    • Salt to taste
    • Water as needed
  • For Chokha:
    • 2 medium-sized brinjals (eggplants)
    • 2 medium-sized tomatoes
    • 2-3 medium-sized potatoes
    • 2-3 green chilies
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2-3 tbsp mustard oil
    • Salt to taste
    • Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. For Litti:
    1. In a mixing bowl, combine whole wheat flour, salt, and ghee. Mix well.
    2. Add water gradually to form a soft dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
    3. In another bowl, mix sattu, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, ajwain, and salt.
    4. Divide the dough into small balls, flatten them, and fill with the sattu mixture. Seal the edges and shape them into balls.
    5. Preheat the oven or tandoor. Bake the littis until golden brown and cooked through, about 20-25 minutes.
