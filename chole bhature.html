Chole Bhature Recipe

Ingredients

For Chole:

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

For Bhature:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup semolina (optional, for crispiness)
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

For Chole:

  1. Pressure cook the soaked chickpeas with a little salt and water until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  6. Mix in turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
  7. Add the cooked chickpeas along with some water and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.

For Bhature:

  1. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, semolina, salt, and baking soda.
  2. Add curd and knead into a soft dough, adding water if required.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for 2-3 hours.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them into circles.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the rolled dough until golden and puffed.
  6. Serve the hot bhature with chole.

Enjoy your homemade Chole Bhature!
