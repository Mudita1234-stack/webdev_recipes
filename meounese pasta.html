Mayonnaise Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

For the Pasta:

  • 200g pasta (penne, fusilli, or your choice)
  • Water for boiling pasta
  • Salt for boiling water

For the Mayonnaise Sauce:

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream (optional for extra creaminess)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes (optional for spice)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp finely chopped vegetables (carrots, peas, or capsicum)
  • Fresh parsley or coriander for garnish (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Cook the Pasta

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions (usually around 8-10 minutes).
  3. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and set it aside. You can drizzle a little olive oil on the pasta to prevent it from sticking together.

Step 2: Prepare the Mayonnaise Sauce

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mayonnaise, fresh cream (if using), olive oil or butter, black pepper, and dried oregano.
  2. Mix well until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  3. If you want a spicier kick, add the red chili flakes to the sauce and mix well.
  4. Stir in the finely chopped vegetables and mix everything together.
  5. Add salt to taste and adjust the seasoning as desired.

Step 3: Combine Pasta and Sauce

  1. Add the drained pasta into the mayonnaise sauce mixture.
  2. Toss the pasta and sauce gently until the pasta is well-coated with the creamy mayonnaise sauce.
  3. If the sauce seems too thick, you can add a little pasta water to loosen it up.

Step 4: Serve

  1. Once the pasta is evenly coated, transfer it to serving plates.
  2. Garnish with fresh parsley or coriander leaves if desired.
  3. Serve your creamy and delicious Mayonnaise Pasta hot or cold.

Enjoy!

Enjoy your creamy and flavorful Mayonnaise Pasta, a quick and delicious dish that is perfect for any time of the day!
