Manchurian Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the Manchurian Balls:
    • 1 cup grated cabbage
    • 1/2 cup grated carrots
    • 1/2 cup finely chopped beans
    • 1/4 cup finely chopped bell pepper
    • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
    • 1/4 cup cornflour
    • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Oil for frying
  • For the Manchurian Sauce:
    • 2 tablespoons oil
    • 1 tablespoon ginger, finely chopped
    • 1 tablespoon garlic, finely chopped
    • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
    • 1/2 cup bell pepper, chopped
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon vinegar
    • 1 tablespoon chili sauce (adjust to taste)
    • 1 teaspoon cornflour mixed with 2 tablespoons water (for thickening)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine grated cabbage, grated carrots, chopped beans, and bell pepper.
  2. Add all-purpose flour, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well to form a dough-like consistency.
  3. Shape the mixture into small balls (about 1 inch in diameter).
  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Fry the Manchurian balls in batches until they are golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  5. For the sauce, heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan over medium heat.
  6. Add chopped ginger and garlic, and saut√© for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  7. Add chopped onion and bell pepper, and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until they are slightly tender.
  8. Pour in soy sauce, vinegar, and chili sauce. Mix well.
  9. Add the cornflour slurry (cornflour mixed with water) to thicken the sauce. Stir continuously until the sauce thickens. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Add the fried Manchurian balls to the sauce and toss gently to coat them evenly.
  11. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot with fried rice or noodles. Enjoy your delicious Manchurian!
