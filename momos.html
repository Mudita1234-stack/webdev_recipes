Momos Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the Dough:
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • Water, as needed
  • For the Filling:
    • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
    • 1/2 cup finely chopped carrots
    • 1/2 cup finely chopped bell pepper
    • 1/2 cup finely chopped onions
    • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon sesame oil (optional)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour and salt. Gradually add water and knead to form a smooth and soft dough. Cover with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In another bowl, combine chopped cabbage, carrots, bell pepper, onions, ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, pepper, and chopped coriander leaves. Mix well to prepare the filling.
  3. After the dough has rested, divide it into small balls (about the size of a golf ball).
  4. Roll each ball into a thin circle (about 4-5 inches in diameter) on a lightly floured surface.
  5. Place a spoonful of the filling in the center of each circle.
  6. Fold the edges of the dough over the filling to form a half-moon shape. Pinch the edges to seal the momos. You can pleat the edges for a decorative look.
  7. Prepare a steamer by boiling water in a pot. Place the momos in a greased steamer basket, leaving some space between them.
  8. Steam the momos for about 15-20 minutes until they are cooked and translucent.
  9. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce, such as chili sauce or soy sauce. Enjoy your delicious momos!
