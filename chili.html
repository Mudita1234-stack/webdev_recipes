Chilli Paneer Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250 grams paneer, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup cornflour
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • For the Sauce:
    • 2 tablespoons oil
    • 1 tablespoon ginger, finely chopped
    • 1 tablespoon garlic, finely chopped
    • 1 medium onion, sliced
    • 1 bell pepper (capsicum), sliced
    • 2-3 green chilies, slit
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon chili sauce (adjust to taste)
    • 1 tablespoon vinegar
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine cornflour, all-purpose flour, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Add water gradually to make a smooth batter.
  2. Dip the paneer cubes into the batter, ensuring they are well coated.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Fry the paneer cubes in batches until they are golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  4. In another pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil over medium heat.
  5. Add chopped ginger and garlic, and saut√© for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  6. Add sliced onion, bell pepper, and green chilies. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.
  7. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, and sugar. Mix well and cook for another minute.
  8. Add the fried paneer cubes to the sauce and toss gently to coat them evenly.
  9. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot with fried rice or noodles. Enjoy your delicious Chilli Paneer!
