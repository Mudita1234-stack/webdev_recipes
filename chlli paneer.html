Chilli Paneer Recipe

Ingredients

For the Paneer Coating:

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tbsp cornflour (cornstarch)
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed to make a batter)
  • Oil for frying

For the Chilli Paneer Gravy:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper (capsicum), sliced
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp cornflour mixed in 2 tbsp water (cornflour slurry)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Paneer

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine cornflour, all-purpose flour, red chili powder, black pepper, ginger-garlic paste, and salt.
  2. Add a little water to make a thick batter for coating the paneer cubes. The batter should be enough to coat the paneer evenly.
  3. Heat oil in a pan or deep fryer for frying.
  4. Dip each paneer cube into the batter and fry in the hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Once fried, remove the paneer cubes from the oil and set them aside on a paper towel to drain excess oil.

Step 2: Prepare the Gravy

  1. In the same pan, heat 2 tbsp oil over medium heat.
  2. Add sliced onions, bell pepper, and green chilies. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly cooked but still crunchy.
  3. Now, add the ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, sugar, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine all the ingredients.
  5. To thicken the gravy, add the cornflour slurry (cornflour mixed with water) and cook the gravy until it thickens to your desired consistency.

Step 3: Combine the Paneer and Gravy

  1. Once the gravy is thickened, add the fried paneer cubes to the pan.
  2. Gently toss the paneer in the gravy to coat it well with the sauce.
  3. Cook for another 1-2 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

Step 4: Serve

  1. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired.
  2. Serve your delicious Chilli Paneer hot with fried rice, noodles, or even as an appetizer.

Enjoy!
