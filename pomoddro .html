Pomodoro Noodles Recipe

Ingredients

For Noodles:

  • 200g noodles (spaghetti or any type of your choice)
  • Water for boiling noodles
  • Salt to taste

For Pomodoro Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3-4 ripe tomatoes, finely chopped or pureed
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

Step 1: Cook the Noodles

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the noodles and cook according to the package instructions (usually around 8-10 minutes).
  3. Once cooked, drain the noodles and set them aside. Save a little pasta water to adjust the sauce consistency later if needed.

Step 2: Make the Pomodoro Sauce

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant but not browned.
  3. Add the chopped or pureed tomatoes, and cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the tomatoes break down and soften.
  4. Season with dried oregano, sugar (optional), salt, and pepper to taste. Stir well and let the sauce simmer for another 10 minutes until it thickens.
  5. If the sauce gets too thick, you can add a little of the reserved pasta water to reach your desired consistency.

Step 3: Combine Noodles and Sauce

  1. Add the cooked noodles directly into the sauce and toss them well, ensuring the noodles are fully coated with the pomodoro sauce.
  2. Let the noodles and sauce cook together for another 2-3 minutes to absorb the flavors.

Step 4: Serve

  1. Serve the pomodoro noodles hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese if desired.

Enjoy!

Enjoy your homemade Pomodoro Noodles, a simple yet flavorful pasta dish!
