Idli Dosa Sambhar Recipe

Idli Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 2 cups idli rice (or parboiled rice)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions:

  1. Soak urad dal and fenugreek seeds in water for 4-6 hours.
  2. Soak the rice separately for the same duration.
  3. Drain the soaked dal and rice, and blend them separately to a smooth batter, adding water as needed.
  4. Mix both batters together in a large bowl and add salt.
  5. Cover and let it ferment overnight or for 8-12 hours.
  6. Once fermented, pour the batter into idli molds and steam for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambhar.

Dosa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Same batter as idli (fermented batter)
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions:

  1. Heat a non-stick pan or tawa over medium heat.
  2. Pour a ladleful of fermented batter and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin layer.
  3. Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges.
  4. Cook until the edges turn golden brown and crisp.
  5. Flip and cook for another minute.
  6. Serve hot with chutney and sambhar.

Sambhar Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup toor dal (split pigeon peas)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, potato, etc.)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies
  • 1 tsp sambhar powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Coriander leaves for garnish
  • Oil for tempering
  • Mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves for tempering

Instructions:

  1. Cook toor dal in a pressure cooker with enough water until soft. Mash and set aside.
  2. In a pot, heat oil and add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves
