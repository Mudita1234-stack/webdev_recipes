Spring Roll Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the Filling:
    • 1 cup cabbage, finely shredded
    • 1 cup carrots, julienned
    • 1/2 cup bell pepper, thinly sliced
    • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
    • 2 green onions, chopped
    • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
    • 1 tablespoon garlic, minced
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the Spring Rolls:
    • Spring roll wrappers (about 10-12)
    • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat a tablespoon of oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the minced ginger and garlic, and saut√© for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the shredded cabbage, carrots, bell pepper, bean sprouts, and green onions. Stir-fry for about 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender but still crisp.
  4. Pour in the soy sauce and sesame oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir well and cook for another minute. Remove from heat and let the filling cool.
  5. Once the filling has cooled, take a spring roll wrapper and place it on a clean surface or cutting board with one corner facing you.
  6. Place about 2 tablespoons of the filling near the corner closest to you. Fold that corner over the filling, then fold in the sides and roll it up tightly to form a spring roll. Seal the edge with a little water.
  7. Repeat the process with the remaining wrappers and filling.
  8. In a deep frying pan or wok, heat enough oil for deep frying over medium-high heat.
  9. Once the oil is hot, carefully add the spring rolls in batches, frying until they are golden brown and crispy (about 3-4 minutes). Make sure not to overcrowd the pan.
  10. Using a slotted spoon, remove the spring rolls and drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  11. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce, such as sweet chili sauce or soy sauce. Enjoy your delicious spring rolls!
