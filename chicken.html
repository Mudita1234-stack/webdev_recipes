Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

For Chicken Marinade:

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste

For Biryani:

  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked for 30 minutes)
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4-5 green chilies, slit
  • 1 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mint leaves, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 4-5 whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom)
  • 1 cup milk mixed with a pinch of saffron
  • 4 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

  1. In a large bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and coat well. Cover and marinate for at least 1 hour or overnight in the refrigerator.

Step 2: Cook the Rice

  1. Boil water in a large pot with salt and whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom).
  2. Add the soaked basmati rice and cook until 70% done. Drain and set aside.

Step 3: Prepare the Chicken

  1. Heat ghee or oil in a large pan. Add cumin seeds and sliced onions. Sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add green chilies, tomatoes, and mint leaves. Cook until the tomatoes soften.
  3. Add the marinated chicken and cook until the chicken is tender and the oil separates.

Step 4: Layer the Biryani

  1. In a heavy-bottomed pot, spread a layer of chicken masala at the bottom.
  2. Top with a layer of cooked rice.
  3. Sprinkle saffron milk, fried onions, coriander, and mint leaves over the rice.
  4. Repeat the layering process, finishing with rice on top.

Step 5: Dum Cooking

  1. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid. Seal the edges with dough, if necessary.
  2. Cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
  3. Turn off the heat and let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.

Serving

Gently mix the layers and serve hot with raita or salad. Enjoy your aromatic Chicken Biryani!
