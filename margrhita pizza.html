Margherita Pizza Recipe

Ingredients:

  • For the Dough:
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 tsp instant yeast
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 3/4 cup warm water
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • For the Toppings:
    • 1/2 cup pizza sauce (store-bought or homemade)
    • 1 cup fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
    • Fresh basil leaves
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Optional: Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, instant yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil to the dry ingredients. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or baking sheet inside to heat up.
  6. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness (about 1/4 inch thick).
  7. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a piece of parchment paper for easy handling.
  8. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  9. Layer the fresh mozzarella slices on top of the sauce.
  10. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and season with salt and pepper.
  11. Transfer the pizza (with the parchment paper) to the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet.
  12. Bake for about 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  13. Remove the pizza from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
  14. Optional: Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese before serving.
  15. Slice and enjoy your delicious Margherita Pizza!
