Maggi Noodles Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet of Maggi noodles
  • 1 3/4 cups water
  • 1 sachet of Maggi tastemaker (included in the packet)
  • Optional: Vegetables (e.g., peas, carrots, bell peppers), chopped
  • Optional: 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • Optional: 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • Optional: Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, bring 1 3/4 cups of water to a boil.
  2. If using, add the chopped vegetables and green chilies to the boiling water and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add the Maggi noodles to the boiling water. You can break the noodles into halves or quarters if you prefer smaller pieces.
  4. Cook the noodles for about 2 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
  5. Add the Maggi tastemaker sachet to the noodles and mix well.
  6. Continue to cook for another 1-2 minutes, or until the noodles are cooked to your desired consistency.
  7. If you like, add oil or butter for extra flavor and creaminess.
  8. Once done, turn off the heat and let it sit for a minute.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves if desired.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Maggi noodles!
