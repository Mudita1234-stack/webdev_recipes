American Chop Suey Recipe

Ingredients

For the Vegetables:

  • 1 cup cabbage, finely shredded
  • 1/2 cup carrots, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup capsicum (bell peppers), finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup French beans, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup peas (optional)

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup tomato ketchup
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce (optional for heat)
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Water (as needed for consistency)

For the Noodles:

  • 200g noodles (preferably hakka noodles)
  • Water for boiling noodles
  • Salt for boiling noodles

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Noodles

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add salt.
  2. Once the water starts boiling, add the noodles and cook according to the package instructions (usually 7-8 minutes).
  3. Drain the noodles, rinse them under cold water, and set aside.

Step 2: Prepare the Vegetables

  1. Heat oil in a large wok or pan over medium heat.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add all the chopped vegetables (cabbage, carrots, capsicum, French beans, peas) and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until they are slightly cooked but still crunchy.
  4. Ensure the vegetables do not become overcooked, as you want them to retain their texture.

Step 3: Prepare the Sauce

  1. In a separate small bowl, mix tomato ketchup, soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, sugar, and black pepper.
  2. Add the sauce mixture to the cooked vegetables in the wok and stir well to coat the vegetables.
  3. If the sauce seems too thick, add a little water to adjust the consistency.

Step 4: Combine Noodles and Vegetables

  1. Add the boiled noodles to the wok with vegetables and sauce.
  2. Gently toss everything together until the noodles are well-coated with the sauce and vegetables.
  3. Stir-fry the noodles for another 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to combine.

Step 5: Serve

  1. Once everything is well mixed and heated through, serve your American Chop Suey hot.
  2. You can garnish with some spring onions or fresh coriander for an added touch.

Enjoy!

American Chop Suey is now ready to be served! Enjoy this delicious, tangy, and spicy noodle dish!
