Hakka Noodles Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200 grams Hakka noodles (or any other noodles)
  • 2 tablespoons oil (vegetable or sesame oil)
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper (capsicum), thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1/4 cup spring onions (scallions), chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar (white or rice vinegar)
  • 1 teaspoon chili sauce (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1/2 cup cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu for protein

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a large pot. Add a pinch of salt and cook the Hakka noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain the noodles and rinse them under cold water to stop the cooking process. Set aside.
  2. In a large wok or frying pan, heat the oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chopped ginger and garlic, and saut√© for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add the sliced onions and stir-fry until they become translucent.
  5. Next, add the julienned carrot, bell pepper, and shredded cabbage. Stir-fry for about 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender but still crisp.
  6. If using, add the cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu at this stage and stir-fry for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  7. Add the cooked noodles to the pan. Pour in the soy sauce, vinegar, and chili sauce. Toss everything together gently to combine, ensuring the noodles are well coated with the sauces.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through.
  9. Finally, add the chopped spring onions and give it a quick toss.
  10. Remove from heat and serve hot. Enjoy your delicious Hakka noodles!
